The Himalayaa are a mountain range located in the far north of India. They hold the largest mountains in the world.


Yellowstone National Park is a national park located in the northwest of the United States. It is a beautiful park that holds many animals, such a bison and Grizzly Bears. It is a park that rests on top of one of very few supervolcanos in the world. It likely won't erupt in the near future, but if it does, 80% of the US will be gone in minutes.


Mt St Helens is a volcano located in the state of Washinton in the US. It last erupted in 1980.


The Rocky Mountains are a mountain range located in the North American continent. They are some of the best looking terrain you'll find in North America.


Niagara Falls is a massive waterfall famous for its looks. It is located in Canada.


The Pyramids of Giza are a set of Pyramids contructed of 15 ton blocks of sand and stone that are located in Egypt. They were contructed 4000 - 5000 years ago, making them among the most ancient mega structures ever built.
